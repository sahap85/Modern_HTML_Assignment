Accessibility in Web Development: 
Accessibility in web development refers to designing and coding websites
in a way that ensures people with disabilities can perceive, understand, navigate, and interact with digital
content effectively. It involves making web content inclusive and usable for everyone, regardless of their
abilities.

Importance of Accessible Websites:
1. Increased website traffic
2. Reduced risk of legal complications
3. More customers
4. Helps develop an innovative business mindset
5. Improved website SEO
6.Better user journey leading to more conversions
